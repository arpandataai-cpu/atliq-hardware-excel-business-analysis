# 📊 AtliQ Hardware Sales Analysis using Microsoft Excel

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Sales Analytics solution developed using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful dashboards and business insights to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer performance
- Evaluate market performance against targets
- Analyze Profit & Loss (P&L)
- Identify revenue concentration risk
- Identify high-growth customers
- Generate business recommendations

---

## 🛠 Tools & Skills Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Power Pivot
- DAX
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Conditional Formatting
- Business Analytics

---

## 📈 Reports Included

### Original Reports
- Customer Performance Report
- Market Performance vs Target
- P&L Statement by Fiscal Year
- P&L Statement by Market
- P&L Statement by Month

### Additional Business Analysis
- Revenue Concentration Analysis
- Customer Growth Analysis

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Top customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.
- High-growth customers represent future business opportunities.
- Market and customer performance vary across business segments.
- Data-driven reporting supports better strategic decisions.
